Pioneering the Fusion of Technology and Environmental Stewardship in Nepal

ATERN is dedicated to integrating advanced technology with environmental research to create sustainable solutions for Nepal's unique challenges and contribute to global conservation efforts.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, ATERN began as a small team of passionate researchers and technologists concerned about Nepal's environmental challenges. From our humble beginnings in Kathmandu, we've grown into a leading organization driving innovation in green technology and sustainable practices.

Our journey has been marked by groundbreaking projects, from AI-powered wildlife monitoring to blockchain-based carbon credit systems, always with the goal of preserving Nepal's rich biodiversity while advancing technological capabilities.
